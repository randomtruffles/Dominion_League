The Dominion League is run by a team of volunteer moderators.

Ways to contact us:

Dominion Discord
Join the Dominion Online discord (https://discord.gg/vMmmMBu).
There is a channel called #league where you can post questions. The discord is also a great community for you to chat about dominion strategy, join other tournaments and more.

League Hotline Form
Dominion League Hotline Form.
Fill out this form if you have any issues in the Dominion League! Your message will go to all Dominion League moderators, who will follow up with you ASAP. Make sure to check that your questions aren't already answered in the F.A.Q. or the rules.

E-mail
You can e-mail us at dominionleaguemoderator@gmail.com

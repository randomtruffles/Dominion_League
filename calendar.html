Upcoming Dominion Online Matches

This is a calendar for upcoming announced matches on Dominion Online in organized tournaments, including the Dominion League and the Dominion World Cup. The default time zone is UTC. If you use Google Calendar, you can add it to your own by clicking the plus sign in the lower-right corner.

How to spectate a match on Dominion Online

  1. Friend one of the players in the match on the client . (Friend List > Add)
  2. Go back to Matching, find their name under Friends on the right and click the join button to spectate their game.
    • If they don't appear, repeat steps 1. and 2. with the other player or a known spectator as the player may have Followers off.
  3. Check that the players' ability to see spectator chat is off before you type any game sensitive information. If the chat box (where you type) says:
    • message spectators: players cannot see spectator chat.
    • message: players will be able to see the chat.
